Cristiano Ronaldo (Al Nassr/Portugal) - 53 goals\n\nRonaldo has chipped in with 43 goals for Al Nassr to prove he never left his shooting boots back in Europe.\n\nHe has even banged in ten times for Portugal, as he helped the Euro 2016 winners triumph in all ten of their Euro 2024 qualifying matches.\n\nHis half-century in 2023 is the eighth time in his career that he has scored at least 50 goals in a calendar year.\n\nHis two strikes in the 5-2 win against Karim Benzema's Al Ittihad saw him leapfrog above Kane and Mbappe.\n\nAnd with Al Nassr still having one match left before 2024, he could add to his ever-growing tally."
