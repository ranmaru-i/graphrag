Contacts.\"\n\nThe program was set to begin on May 1, 2020 — and that timing would turn out to be rather providential.\n\n\"We had no idea when we started that COVID was around the corner and the department at Children's Nebraska is offered the chance to participate in Caring Contacts, said Schalley.\n\nAbout 98% of families agree to join the program, she said.\n\nnext Image 1 of 6\n\nprev
